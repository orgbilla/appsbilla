ContractionTimer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Summary

Your data never leaves your device. ContractionTimer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Period.

Data Collection

ContractionTimer collects zero personal data. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Health or medical data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

Data Storage

All contraction timing data is stored locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data is not synced to any cloud service, server, or third party.

Third-Party Services

ContractionTimer does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Medical Disclaimer

ContractionTimer is a timing tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The hospital alert feature uses general guidelines (such as the 5-1-1 rule) and should not replace the specific instructions of your healthcare provider. Always consult your doctor, midwife, or medical professional regarding labor and delivery decisions.

Children's Privacy

ContractionTimer does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page. Given that we collect no data, changes are unlikely.
